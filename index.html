About Me

My name is Noriah Williams and I am currently a Client Support Specialist at OxBlue Corporation. I graduated from Michigan State University in the spring of 2013 with a Bachelor of Arts in Journalism. I am pursing a career in Web Development and am in enrolled in the Coding Bootcamp at Georgia Tech, set to graduate February 2018. I have a passion for sharing information through various platforms and throughout my career have worked in print media, public relations and customer support.
